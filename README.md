# Kevin Heleodoro

I am a dedicated software engineer with over 3 years of experience in developing scalable and maintainable software solutions. Currently, I am a Staff Engineer at Booz Allen Hamilton, where I enhance client business applications, improve processes, and create DevSecOps pipelines. My role also includes conducting code reviews, researching new technologies, and planning innovative solutions.

Previously, I taught software engineering fundamentals to transitioning service members at Galvanize, Inc., which sharpened my mentoring skills. My military background in the U.S. Army emphasized leadership and teamwork, as I managed and mentored team members.

I hold a Master's in Computer Science from Northeastern University, and my academic background also includes a Bachelor's in Business Administration and Management and an Associate of Arts. I am passionate about web development, video games, and blockchain technology. I continually explore new advancements and innovative solutions to stay ahead in the field.
